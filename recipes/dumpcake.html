Southern Dump Cake

Ingredients

  • Cherry pie filling -- 1 21 oz can
  • Crushed pineapple -- 2 8 oz cans
  • Yellow cake mix -- 1 package
  • Butter -- 1/2 cup (1 stick)

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Stir cherry pie filling and crushed pineapple together in a 9x13-inch pan and spread in an even layer.
  3. Sprinkle dry cake mix evenly over pineapple and cherry mixture.
  4. drizzle butter on top, making sure minimal dry spots remain.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown on top, about 35 to 40 minutes. Enjoy!
